🎉 2025 Tax Year Updates

Great news! Lower tax rates (25%/35%), higher personal allowance ($130,000 monthly), and new qualification allowances for degree holders are now in effect.

2025 Tax Rates
  • 25% on income up to $260,000 monthly
  • 35% on income above $260,000 monthly
  • Reduced from previous 28%/40% rates
Key Allowances
  • Personal: $130,000 or 1/3 gross income
  • Child: $10,000 per child monthly
  • Overtime: First $50,000 non-taxable
  • Second Job: First $50,000 non-taxable
Deductions
  • NIS: 5.6% up to $280,000 ceiling
  • Insurance: Up to 10% gross or $50,000
  • Gratuity: 22.5% basic salary (non-taxable)
New: Qualification Allowances
  • ACCA: $15,000 monthly
  • Master's: $22,000 monthly
  • Doctoral: $32,000 monthly
  • Effective January 2025

Annual Package Structure

Your annual package typically consists of: Net salary + 6 months Gratuity + Vacation allowance. Gratuity is paid twice a year (every 6 months), while vacation allowance is included with your annual package. The calculator will automatically distribute these payments appropriately when calculating monthly and annual figures.

Allowances Information

Gratuity Information

Gratuity is calculated at the rate of 22.5% of basic salary per month and accumulated over time. It is typically paid at 6-month intervals. Gratuity payments are not subject to income tax in Guyana.
